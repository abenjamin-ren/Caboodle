Student (Core)

~ learner, pupil, child, kid

A Student is an individual learner enrolled in one or more educational organizations who engages in instructional activities, completes assessments and assignments, and serves as the primary anchor for all learning-related data, progress tracking, and outcomes across Renaissance products.

Business Rules

Constraints and policies that govern how Student behaves across the system.

  1. Uniqueness — A Student is uniquely identified within a District by their Student ID. State Student IDs provide cross-district matching.
  2. Creation — Students are primarily created via SIS roster sync (OneRoster, Clever, ClassLink). Manual creation requires at minimum: First Name, Last Name, Grade Level.
  3. Enrollment — A Student must be enrolled in at least one School to be active. They may belong to zero or more Classes.
  4. Data Privacy — Student PII (date of birth, demographics) is restricted to Admin roles. Teachers see name, grade, and academic data. FERPA and COPPA compliance required.
  5. Transfer — When a Student transfers schools, historical Scores and progress data travel with them. The student is removed from their previous school's active roster but historical records persist.
  6. Deactivation — Deactivated students are hidden from active rosters and reports but their data is retained for historical reporting and can be reactivated.
  7. Score Accumulation — Scores are append-only. Once generated, a Score cannot be deleted (only excluded from certain reports by Admins).

Relationship Specs (MCSFD)

How Student connects to other objects: Mechanics, Cardinality, Sorts, Filters, and Dependencies.

sync_alt Student ↔ Class
MechanicsStudents are enrolled in Classes primarily via SIS roster sync (automated). Admins can also manually add/remove students.
CardinalityMany-to-many. A Student can belong to multiple Classes; a Class has multiple Students. Typical: 1-8 classes per student, 15-35 students per class.
SortsStudents within a Class: alphabetical by last name (default), by grade level, by reading/math level.
FiltersBy grade level, by enrollment status (active/inactive), by demographic group, by proficiency level.
DependenciesIf a Class is archived, students remain in the system but lose their class association for that class. Historical data persists.
sync_alt Student ↔ Score
MechanicsScores are system-generated when a Student completes an Assessment. Auto-calculated by the assessment engine.
CardinalityOne-to-many. A Student accumulates many Scores over time; each Score belongs to exactly one Student.
SortsBy date (most recent first, default), by assessment type, by subject area, by score value.
FiltersBy assessment type (Star, DnA, FastBridge), by subject (Reading, Math, SEL), by date range, by benchmark category.
DependenciesIf a Student is deactivated, Scores persist for historical reporting but are excluded from active aggregations.
sync_alt Student ↔ School
MechanicsStudents are assigned to a School via SIS roster sync. Transfer is handled by District Admins or system sync.
CardinalityMany-to-one (at a given time). A Student belongs to one School; a School contains many Students.
SortsStudents within a School: alphabetical (default), by grade level.
FiltersBy grade, by class, by enrollment status, by teacher.
DependenciesIf a School is deactivated, students must be transferred to another School or deactivated.
